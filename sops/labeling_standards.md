# Labeling Standards

## Purpose

To maintain food safety, traceability, allergen awareness, and production organization.

## Required Label Information

- Item name
- Preparation date
- Use-by date
- Initials of preparer
- Allergen notes when applicable
- Texture or diet modification when applicable

## Example Label

```text
Item: Chicken & Rice Casserole
Prepared: 2026-05-14
Use By: 2026-05-17
Prepared By: BG
Diet: Regular
Allergens: Milk, Wheat
