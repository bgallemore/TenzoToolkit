# Reheating Procedure

## Purpose

To standardize safe reheating of prepared foods before service.

## Procedure

1. Verify item name, preparation date, and storage condition.
2. Reheat rapidly using approved equipment.
3. Stir or rotate product to avoid cold spots.
4. Verify internal temperature before service.
5. Record final temperature when required.
6. Discard food that does not meet safety standards.
