# Cooling Procedure

## Purpose

To ensure cooked foods are cooled safely for later service in an institutional kitchen environment.

## Standard

Potentially hazardous foods should be cooled rapidly using shallow pans, ice baths, blast chilling when available, and frequent temperature monitoring.

## Procedure

1. Portion hot food into shallow pans.
2. Leave pans uncovered or loosely covered during active cooling.
3. Place pans in designated cooling area.
4. Stir dense foods periodically.
5. Record temperatures on the cooling log.
6. Cover, label, date, and store once cooling target is reached.

## Documentation

Use `templates/cooling_log_template.csv`.

## Notes

This SOP is intended as a portfolio documentation sample and should be adapted to local regulatory requirements.
